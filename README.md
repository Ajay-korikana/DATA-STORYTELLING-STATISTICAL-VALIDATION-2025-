# DATA-STORYTELLING-STATISTICAL-VALIDATION-2025-
📊 FMCG Sales Analysis — Andhra Pradesh (2025)
🚀 Project Overview

This project analyzes FMCG sales data to uncover key business insights, customer behavior patterns, and growth opportunities.

The goal was to move beyond basic analysis and deliver:

📈 Actionable business insights
🧠 Customer segmentation (RFM)
🔁 Retention analysis (Cohorts)
📊 Interactive dashboard
📉 Statistical validation (Hypothesis Testing)
📁 Dataset Summary
🧾 90,866 transactions
💰 ₹9.54 Crore total revenue
👥 1,219+ customers
🛒 19 product categories
📅 Jan – Dec 2025
🎯 Key Business Insights
💡 Revenue Insights
🚬 Cigarettes contribute ~37% of total revenue
📊 Top 3 categories contribute ~75% of revenue
📈 Strong seasonality — revenue peaks in December (~₹1.2 Cr)
👥 Customer Insights
🏆 278 Champion customers drive majority revenue
⚠️ High dependency on a small customer base
📉 Risk of revenue drop if key customers churn
🔁 Retention Insights
✅ January cohort shows strong retention (~88%)
❌ March & May cohorts show weak retention (~28%)
📊 Indicates inconsistency in customer acquisition quality
⚙️ Operational Insights
💳 ~69% revenue from credit sales (risk factor)
🎯 Discount rate is extremely low (~0.12%)
✅ Data is clean and reliable for analysis
